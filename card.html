Cheetah

Cheetah's are the world's fastest land animal. They can run up to 75mph!

  • Scientific Name: Acinonyx jubatus
  • Average Height: 2.2 – 3.1 ft.
  • Average Lifespan: 10 - 15 years
  • Habitat: Africa

The cheetah is characterised by a slender body, deep chest, spotted coat, a small rounded head, black tear-like streaks on the face, long thin legs and a long spotted tail. Its lightly built, slender form is in sharp contrast with the robust build of the big cats, making it more similar to the cougar. The cheetah reaches nearly 70 to 90 cm (28 to 35 in) at the shoulder, and weighs 21–72 kg (46–159 lb). Though taller than the leopard, it is notably smaller than the lion. Basically yellowish tan or rufous to greyish white, the coat is uniformly covered with nearly 2,000 solid black spots.
